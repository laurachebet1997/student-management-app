Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new student?
Go to Students → Add New Student and fill in the required details.
How do I enroll a student in a course?
Navigate to Enroll to Course in the sidebar, select the student and the desired courses, then click Enroll.
Can a student be enrolled in multiple courses?
Yes — the enrollment form supports multi-select so you can enroll a student in several courses at once.
Who do I contact if I encounter a system error?
Contact any of the system administrators listed above and include a screenshot of the error for faster resolution.
